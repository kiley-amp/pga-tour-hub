🚀 What We're Working On Together
🏌️
PGA Tour Pass — Membership Analytics & Engagement
Tracking the full PGA Tour Pass launch and its impact on authenticated user growth toward the 1M MAU goal. Building dashboards for membership acquisition, engagement, and retention — with a focus on converting the ~200–300 monthly run-rate to a meaningful growth curve by year end.
DRI: Kiley + Matt Mullen 📊 PGA Tour Pass Dashboard
🎯
Unlocking Predictive Audiences — 6 Cohorts Ready to Activate
Six prediction cohorts have been built in Amplitude — including Authentication Probability and Follow Player Probability — and are ready to be activated with live syncs and experiments. Turning these on connects behavioral prediction directly to Airship campaigns and Braze messaging.
DRI: Sam + Kiley + Stephen Hill Status: Ready to activate 📊 View Syncs
🔗
Closing the Braze Conversion Loop
Braze is live as a destination for daily audience syncs. The next step is flowing conversion data back into Amplitude so the full campaign-to-conversion story is measurable — and so PGA Tour can see exactly which Amplitude-driven audiences are driving the most value in Braze.
DRI: Sam + Shessvy Impact: Full ROI visibility for all Braze campaigns
🤖
AI Analytics — Deeper Adoption Across the Team
PGA Tour's AI use cases were highlighted as a company win at Amplitude's All Hands in April 2026. Building on Andy Panjic's early momentum, the goal is to expand AI-powered analysis to the broader product and marketing team — and walk through specific AI use cases for fan engagement and personalization.
Champion: Andy Panjic Featured: Amplitude All Hands, Apr 2026
✈️
Journey Orchestration — Evaluating for Fan Engagement
Amplitude's Journey Orchestration enables multi-step behavioral campaigns triggered by real-time events — a natural fit for tournament-week messaging, player-following journeys, and PGA Tour Pass onboarding. We're building a comparison and initial use case framework to evaluate this alongside Braze Canvas.
DRI: Nitin Sethi + Shessvy Status: Evaluation in progress
🖥️
Microsoft Teams Integration + MCP Exploration
Teams integration is underway — working through authentication configuration with Scott Colter and Keaton Tucker. Additionally exploring Amplitude's MCP capabilities for deeper integration with PGA Tour's existing developer workflows.
DRI: Scott Colter (PGA) Status: Auth in progress

Featured at Amplitude All Hands — AI Win
PGA Tour's AI use cases were spotlighted company-wide at Amplitude's April 2026 All Hands as a customer success story — a testament to the team's early and meaningful adoption of AI-powered analytics.
🏌️
Players Championship — 112K Authenticated Users
The PGA Tour Pass launch in March 2026 drove 112,000 authenticated users during The Players Championship. Amplitude provided real-time visibility into the full engagement and authentication funnel throughout the event.
📊
2026 Digital Goals Dashboard — Live
Built with Matt Mullen — a single Amplitude dashboard tracking all of PGA Tour's 2026 digital KPIs, from authenticated user growth to content engagement and membership metrics.
📲
40–50 Personalized Push Notification Campaigns/Month
PGA Tour runs 40–50 active Amplitude-powered audience segments monthly to Airship — covering player-following fans, tournament-specific audiences, re-engagement cohorts, and geographic segments. A mature, high-impact personalization program.

Andy Panjic — AI Power User & Internal Champion
Andy Panjic has become PGA Tour's internal AI champion, driving Amplitude AI adoption across the product team. Regularly pressure-testing new features and leading the charge on AI-powered analysis workflows.
